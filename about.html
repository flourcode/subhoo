About Subhoo

Purpose

Subhoo aims to provide clarity and insight into the complex world of U.S. federal government subcontracting. By visualizing the flow of contract dollars from awarding agencies through prime contractors down to subcontractors, we hope to empower businesses, researchers, and the public with actionable intelligence.

This tool is particularly designed for salespeople and business development professionals seeking to understand agency spending patterns, identify key prime contractors within specific agencies or NAICS codes, discover potential partners or competitors, and track contracting opportunities.

Data Source

All data presented in Subhoo is sourced directly from publicly available datasets provided by USAspending.gov, the official source for U.S. federal spending information. Data is downloaded periodically and processed for visualization.

While we strive for accuracy, the visualizations reflect the data as reported on USAspending.gov at the time of our last update. Dates, amounts, and relationships may change. For definitive contract details, always refer to the official records on USAspending.gov via the provided links.

Technology

Subhoo is built using standard web technologies:

  • HTML5 for structure.
  • CSS3 for styling, utilizing CSS variables for theming.
  • JavaScript for interactivity and data processing.
  • D3.js for creating the data visualizations (Sankey, Network, Map, Donut, Bar charts).
  • PapaParse for reading CSV data.
  • TopoJSON for map data processing.
