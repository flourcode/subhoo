About Subhoo

What This Is

Subhoo is a free tool for federal sales and BD folks who’ve ever been asked, “Who’s the sub?” or “Who else is in the deal?” It maps prime-to-subcontractor relationships using data from USAspending.gov—so you can find partners, spot competitors, and go into meetings sharper.

It started as something I built for myself after years of prospecting the hard way—clicking through tabs on SAM.gov and USAspending just to answer basic questions. Now it’s a tool anyone can use.

If you’re only talking to one contact at one prime, you’re probably single-threaded. That means your deal can vanish with a reorg, a re-compete, or someone forgetting to CC you on the strategy call. Subhoo helps you map the full picture—starting with which **Agency Office** is spending the money, who they’re awarding it to, and which subs are already supporting the work.

This kind of visibility lets you form stronger joint opportunities and avoid blind spots in your account plans. As Jeb Blount says in Fanatical Prospecting, “The number one reason for empty pipelines is the failure to consistently prospect.” Subhoo gives you a smarter starting point—so you’re not just working harder, but aiming better too.

Where the Data Comes From

Everything comes from 100% public records on USAspending.gov. I just clean it up and make it visual. Files are updated monthly. Nothing here is behind a login or scraped—it's all just easier to see so you can get to the right people faster.

Want to double-check a detail? I link back to the source when possible.
