About Subhoo

Illuminating Federal Contracting Relationships

Our Mission

Subhoo was created with a simple but powerful mission: to bring transparency to the complex world of federal contracting relationships. We believe that understanding the connections between prime contractors and subcontractors should be accessible to everyone in the government contracting ecosystem.

By visualizing the flow of federal dollars from agencies through primes to subcontractors, we help business development professionals identify potential teaming partners, track competitor relationships, and find new opportunities that might otherwise remain hidden.

Data & Methodology

All data presented on Subhoo comes directly from USAspending.gov, the official source for spending data for the U.S. Government. We focus specifically on subcontracting data, which reveals the relationships between prime contractors and their subcontractors.

Our approach includes:

  • Monthly Updates: We refresh our database monthly to ensure you have access to the most current information available.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: We include all available subcontracting data from USAspending.gov across all federal agencies.
  • Multiple Visualizations: We offer contract flow diagrams, network charts, and geographic maps to help you analyze the data from different perspectives.
  • Filtering Capabilities: Our powerful filtering system allows you to focus on specific agencies, contractors, or NAICS codes.

For data accuracy questions or to report discrepancies, please refer to the original source data on USAspending.gov.

Why We Created Subhoo

After years working in the federal contracting space, I noticed a significant gap in the availability of subcontracting intelligence. While prime contract awards are widely reported and analyzed, the critical subcontractor relationships that drive much of the actual work remained largely hidden from view.

Subhoo was built to solve this problem by making subcontracting data not just accessible, but genuinely useful through intuitive visualizations and powerful filtering tools. Our goal is to level the playing field for all participants in the federal marketplace.

As a 100% free resource, Subhoo is my contribution to improving the efficiency and transparency of the federal contracting ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?

We update our database monthly with the latest information available from USAspending.gov.

Why don't I see all contracts for a specific agency?

Subhoo focuses specifically on subcontracting data. Not all prime contracts have reported subcontracts, and some may fall below reporting thresholds. Additionally, contracts without subcontracting activity won't appear in our visualizations.

Can I export the data I find?

Yes, Subhoo offers CSV export functionality for most data views, including detailed contract information and expiring contracts listings.

Is there an API available?

Currently, we don't offer a public API. If you have specific data integration needs, please reach out directly to discuss potential options.

How can I provide feedback or report issues?
