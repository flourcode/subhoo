About Subhoo

Subhoo shows you the hidden partnerships behind Federal Primes and Subcontractors so you can, follow the money, find your next teaming partner, or see who's working with your competitors. Pick an agency to start. All data is sourced from USASpending.gov and updated monthly.Users can explore these connections by agency, follow financial flows, identify potential teaming partners, and analyze competitor relationships.

How to Use

  • Select an agency
  • Choose a visualization type
  • Use filters to refine data
  • Click nodes/map areas to focus

Data Source

All data from USAspending.gov. Updated periodically.
